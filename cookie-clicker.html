Unit 6 Concepts

Concepts Covered

Adding An Upgrade Button

Upgrade buttons enhance gameplay by allowing players to spend cookies to unlock features or boost their cookie production rate. This adds a strategic element to the game.

Having the Cookie Grow/Shrink

This concept involves changing the size of the cookie based on player interaction, making the game visually engaging. It can signal that the player has clicked on the cookie or achieved a milestone.

Having the Cookie Move Around

By implementing movement for the cookie, you can create a dynamic challenge for the player. It encourages faster clicks and adds a playful element to the game.

A Golden Cookie Worth Lots

This special cookie appears randomly and provides a substantial reward when clicked. However, it disappears quickly, adding urgency and excitement to gameplay.

A Grandma Who Makes Cookies For You Every Second

This feature allows players to accumulate cookies passively over time. By spending 500 cookies to unlock a grandma, players can enjoy a steady income of cookies every second.
