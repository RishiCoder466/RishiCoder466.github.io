Unit 8 Concepts

Concepts Covered

Adding a Description to the Product

This concept involves allowing the user to add a description of the product they are selling. It gives more details about the product, helping customers make better purchasing decisions.

Adding Quality Textbox and Cost Textbox

The quality and cost textboxes allow the seller to specify the quality of the product and its price. These inputs help the system define product characteristics and make the purchasing process smoother.

Adding an Add to Cart Button

Implementing an "Add to Cart" button allows customers to add the selected product to their shopping cart. This button is an essential part of the online shopping experience.

Adding a Credit Card Number Textbox

The credit card number textbox enables customers to securely enter their payment information, which is critical for completing an online purchase.

Adding a Submit Payment Button

The submit payment button is used to process the payment after the customer has entered their credit card information. It triggers the backend to charge the customer's card and complete the transaction.

Creating a Payment Submitted Page

The payment submitted page is displayed after the customer successfully submits their payment. It confirms the order and thanks the customer for their purchase.
